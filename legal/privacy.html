2. What we collect

2.1 Account and sign-in data

If you sign in using Google, we receive limited information such as your name and email address.

If you purchase a service or place an order, we may collect information needed to fulfill it, such as:

  • Order details (what you purchased)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our sites. This may collect device and usage data (for example, pages viewed, clicks, approximate location, device type).

3. How we use information

We use data to:

We process data to perform our contract with you, meet legal obligations, protect our legitimate interests (fraud prevention, security, operations), and where applicable, with your consent.

5. Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

6. Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for operations, support, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No system is 100 percent secure.

8. Your choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Some data must be retained for legal and operational reasons.
